## **Dragon's Eye**

| **Type** | Capital City |
| **Continent** | Dracordia |
| **Government** | World Government (Seat of Power) |
| **Primary Power Source** | Aetherium |
| **Notable Factions** | World Government , The Rising |


**Dragon's Eye** is the capital city of the continent of Dracordia and the central seat of power for the World Government. Widely considered the heart of modern civilization, it is a global hub for commerce, politics, and Aetherium-powered technology. The city is a breathtaking marvel of engineering, but it is also a nexus of the world's most extreme inequality, representing both the pinnacle of progress and the depths of social disparity.

#### **Geography and Architecture**

Dragon's Eye is a city of extreme verticality, defined by two distinct, co-existing regions: the Upper City and the Undercity.

- **The Upper City:** The "topsider" view of Dragon's Eye is one of pristine order and technological wonder. Gleaming spires of brass and white stone pierce the clouds, connected by elegant sky-bridges used by the city's elite. Magnificent airships dock at massive, glowing towers, and the streets are clean, well-lit by stable Aetherium lamps, and patrolled by the disciplined city guard. This is the world of the wealthy and powerful.
    
- **The Undercity:** Built within the massive foundational caverns and chasms beneath the Upper City is the Undercity, a sprawling, multi-level shantytown. It is a chaotic and hazardous environment, lit by the eerie glow of bioluminescent fungi, leaking chemical runoff, and salvaged, flickering Aetherium tech. The architecture is ad-hoc, a maze of scrap-metal homes, rickety catwalks, and repurposed industrial pipes. The Undercity is home to the city's poor, outcast, and forgotten populations, a stark representation of the class divide.
    
#### **Government and Society**

As the seat of the World Government, Dragon's Eye is the most heavily controlled location in Thaia. A rigid class structure is strictly enforced, with the wealthy living lives of privilege in the spires while the poor toil in the industrial sectors and the Undercity below. This deep-seated inequality has made the city a fertile recruiting ground for The Rising, a revolutionary movement dedicated to overthrowing the established order. The city guard acts as the enforcers of the status quo, while revolutionary cells operate in the shadows of the Undercity.

### **Notable Locations**

- **The Grand Plaza:** A massive, open plaza in the Upper City that serves as the symbolic heart of the World Government.
    
- **The Founder's Statue:** A colossal bronze monument located in the Grand Plaza, depicting the three founders of the World Government. Its base is powered by a glowing Aetherium core, making it a symbol of the government's mastery over technology. It was the site of the assassination of Minister Valerius.
    
- **The Sunken Market:** The chaotic central bazaar and social hub of the Undercity, located on the cavern floor.
    
- **The Chem-Lows:** A dangerous industrial district of the Undercity, known for its toxic environment and rivers of alchemical waste.
    
- **The Workshop:** A large Aether-mechanics workshop in the industrial sector of the Undercity. It is run by the revolutionary leader Sabo and serves as a community hub and a secret base for The Rising.
    
- **The Drowned Rat:** A hidden, well-guarded speakeasy in the Undercity, known as a place to get information.
