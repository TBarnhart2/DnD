(Pray-a)

Preya is a sprawling expanse of plains, dotted with vibrant cities and bustling trade routes. Known for its vast open spaces, fertile lands, and a deep respect for tradition, Preya is a land of vibrant cultures and ancient lore.

- **Main Imports:** Exotic spices, fine textiles, and luxury goods from coastal cities and far-flung lands.
- **Main Exports:** Grains, livestock, and high-quality leather goods.

**Aetheric Applications:**

- **Aetheric Agriculture:** Aetheric technology is used to enhance crop yields, control pests, and even manipulate weather patterns, ensuring bountiful harvests even in times of drought.
- **Aetheric Transportation:** While not as reliant on airships as other regions, Preya utilizes Aetheric-powered carriages and wagons to traverse the vast plains, making travel faster and more efficient.
- **Aetheric Communication:** A network of Aetheric towers allows for rapid communication across the vast expanse of Preya, connecting distant settlements and facilitating trade.

**Racial Composition:**

Preya is a diverse land, home to humans, halflings, and a significant population of gnomes, known for their ingenuity and their love of tinkering.

**Government:**

Preya is a confederation of independent city-states, each governed by its own unique system. However, a loose alliance, known as the "Preyan Concord," exists, allowing for cooperation on matters of trade, defense, and the maintenance of peace.

Preya, with its vast open spaces, its vibrant cultures, and its harmonious blend of tradition and technology, offers a unique glimpse into the diverse tapestry of life in this world.
