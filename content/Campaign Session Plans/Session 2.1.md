#### Message of Death
In the town center, there will be 7 people laying down in a circle - dead, but their bodies perfectly preserved, laying on their backs reaching out their hands. Each one has the Aetheric marks, which are glowing with life

Underneath all of them is a thin Aetheric crystal web connecting them all. If someone stands in the center of the web and casts any magic or attempts to roll arcana or anything magical, they will immediately see the 7 people suddenly hovering above their bodies, as visions. They will each speak
- **Sunborn**: "We foresaw the coming darkness but could not prevent it. The Wellspring requires balanced representation—this has always been the way."
- **Shadowborn**: "Not all of us sought destruction. Some understood that shadows define the light. The Lens was meant to restore, not obliterate."
- **Whisperwind**: "The paths between worlds grow thin. The Nexus points must be stabilized before approaching the Wellspring, or all will be lost."
- **Moonwhisperer**: "We preserved the knowledge of balance. In our ancestral temple lies the key to understanding the Wellspring's true purpose."
- **Skyward**: "The system was designed with seven keys for seven hearts. No single faction can or should control the flow of Aether."
- **Stonebinder**: "We built the framework that channels the power. The blueprint lies in our sanctuary, showing how all connects to one."
- **Deepdweller**: "Beneath the surface lies the truth. The original corruption came not from within but from beyond—the eighth influence that must be purged."

After this is finished, the visions will end and the veins will remain lit up. (When a nexus is destroyed, the veins will die out)

Finally, the compasses will glow and the needle will point in a new direction.

- **Aetheric Cascade (Recharge 5-6):** The Sentinel unleashes a wave of raw Aetheric energy. Each creature in a 15-foot cone originating from the Sentinel must make a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw. On a failure, a creature takes 14 (4d6) force damage and is pushed 10 feet away from the Sentinel. On a success, a creature takes half the damage and isn't pushed.



## The First Nexus - Whisperwind
As they continue to follow the compass, they will eventually stumble upon the First Nexus. 

As they pass over a mountain, on the horizon they see a shimmering mirage-like phenomenon - a tornado of silver energy stretching into the sky, seemingly suspended between planes of existence. As they get closer, they will eventually be approached by "Zephyra", who is there to give them any information they need. 

Zephyra knows that each nexus needs to be balanced, and that this one is mostly balanced as someone previously balanced it (Tua), but was jumping from nexus to nexus along with other things. Stabilizing lasts about 1 month. They only need to stabilize the stone, and all threats will leave once that is done

#### Stabilization
To stabilize the Nexus, they must enter the Nexus and spend 1 minute stabilizing the central Aetheric node, while the rest of the party fights against enemies that spawn from the walls

The person stabilizing the Nexus must spend their full turn and concentration on making Arcana checks to stabilize the node

Round 1 - 3 Gust Wraiths, 1 Cyclone Sentinel

Round 4 - 2 Gust Wraiths, 1 Cyclone Sentinel

Round 7 - 1 Cyclone Sentinel, 1 Nexus Guardian

Once stabilization happens, all enemies will dissipate

Whoever stabilizes the node will level up


After Stabilization, the Tornado will still exist but look more stable. Zephyra will thank them, and the compass will point them to the next Nexus
