## Backstory
Character background:

- Born into a prestigious life as the daughter of a Jakunta councilman, Alora was raised in the grandeur of elven nobility. Her parents were traditionalists, and meticulously groomed her for a life of diplomacy, wealth, and power. From an early age, she was educated in a courtly manner, history, politics, and the intricate webs of noble alliances. Every step of her life was planned, every action scrutinized, and every dream dictated by the expectations of her family.
    
- But deep in her heart, Alora never felt at home in the golden halls and lush estates of her lineage. While her peer embraced the luxury, she longed for something beyond, something wild, untamed, and radiant. During the moment in the family's vast estate gardens, she would gaze longingly at the forests beyond, feeling the whisper of the wind call her name. She had multiple books about nature
    
- As she grew older, her parents became more insistent on her role in the family legacy. Arranged marriages and alliances loomed on the horizon. Feeling the walls closing in Alora, Devised a plan. She convinced her parents to let her study religious and political philosophy at a new school. But the truth was she had no intention of spending another moment trapped in the life they wanted for her.
    
- Now Alora is traveling the world, seeking to uncover her true purpose. She doesn't try to hide her past and she will tell the truth. She is lying to her parents to do what she wants not what they want.
