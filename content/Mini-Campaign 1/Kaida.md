### Description

Kaida is unmistakably of draconic heritage, though a single glance reveals she is not a pure-blooded Dragonborn. For a four-year-old, she is sturdy, with a body covered in a fine coat of shimmering, bronze-colored scales that feel smooth to the touch, like river stones. Her hands and feet are distinctly draconic, ending in small, dark claws that have not yet hardened.

Her mixed ancestry is most apparent in her face and head. She has the softened, shortened muzzle of a Dragonborn, but it is framed by a feature unheard of among them: a mane of unruly, dark brown human hair, a few strands of which carry a metallic sheen. Small, smooth horn buds are visible just above her temples, partially hidden by her hair.

While her draconic features might seem intimidating, her eyes are her most human trait. They are large and expressive, a warm, molten bronze color, but with soft, round pupils instead of reptilian slits. They convey a depth of emotion and a quiet vulnerability that contrasts sharply with her draconic form.

She continues to wear simple, practical clothes and is never seen without her one treasured possession: a worn-out, stuffed dragon doll with mismatched button eyes, which she clutches for comfort.

### Magic

Kaida is an Aetherborn, an individual born with the innate ability to command the raw power of Aether. Like most born in the current age, she is completely unaware of her own heritage or the scale of her power.

Based on the manifestation of her abilities, she is almost certainly a **Sunborn Aetherborn**.

The Sunborn are known as one of the "good" Aetherborn factions that formed the clandestine organization, The White Lotus. Her powers align perfectly with this heritage:

- Her magic is overwhelmingly restorative and protective, focusing on healing (_Cure Wounds_), rest (_Catnap_), and defense (_Globe of Invulnerability_).
    
- When her power manifests, it is consistently described as a "warm, bronze light," aligning with the solar and life-giving themes of the Sunborn.
    
- Even her more destructive outbursts, such as _Destructive Wave_, deal radiant damage—a hallmark of celestial or solar power.
    

Her immense, uncontrolled power is the reason her mother was so desperate to keep her hidden. A living Sunborn Aetherborn is an incredibly rare and valuable being, one that every major faction in the world—including the White Lotus and the Shadowborn—would go to extreme lengths to control.
