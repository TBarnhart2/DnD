## **Dracordia -** Curio & Consequence

```
Welcome to Curio & Consequence, Dracordia's purveyor of peculiar paraphernalia! Step inside and browse our wares, adventurers. Just be careful what you touch... some things bite.
```

- **The Ever-Burning Candle (Common):** This unassuming candle never burns out. It sheds a soft, warm light equivalent to a regular candle. Perfect for those who are tired of constantly replacing their light sources. _Price: 10 gold pieces_
    
- **The Whispering Coin (Uncommon):** A silver coin with strange symbols etched upon it. When flipped, instead of heads or tails, it whispers a single word, seemingly at random. Sometimes it's helpful, sometimes it's gibberish... who knows? Great for adding a little spice to your decision-making. _Price: 50 gold pieces_
    
- **The Portable Puddle (Rare):** A small, seemingly empty vial. When the cork is removed and the vial is tipped, it spills forth a puddle of pure, clean water, approximately 3 feet in diameter. The puddle remains for 1 hour before evaporating. Excellent for those unexpected dry spells. _Price: 250 gold pieces_
    
- **The Self-Stirring Cauldron (Very Rare):** A small, iron cauldron with a mind of its own. Place ingredients inside, speak the activation word ("Muddle"), and the cauldron will expertly stir and mix your concoctions. Perfect for the busy alchemist (or the lazy cook). _Price: 750 gold pieces_
    
- **The Pocket Dimension Key (Legendary):** This ornate, golden key doesn't unlock any physical door. Instead, it opens a small, personal pocket dimension. Large enough to store a few backpacks or a small chest, this space is accessible only to the key's owner. Ideal for those who need a little extra storage on their adventures. _Price: 2,500 gold pieces_

## **Esari -** Kawa No Misaki

```
Welcome to "Kawa no Misaki," Esari's emporium of enchanting enigmas! Step inside and peruse our peculiar provisions, honored guest. But tread carefully... some treasures have teeth.
```

**Our Current Stock:**

1. **The Ever-Folding Fan (Common):** This elegantly crafted fan, made of delicate paper and bamboo, never tears or creases. It provides a gentle breeze and is perfect for adding a touch of flair to any outfit. _Price: 15 silver pieces_
    
2. **The Fortune Cat's Coin (Uncommon):** A small, golden coin featuring a beckoning cat. When held and a question is asked, the cat's paw will point in a direction – perhaps offering guidance, or perhaps just a random gesture. A delightful trinket for those seeking a little whimsy in their lives. _Price: 75 silver pieces_
    
3. **The Bonsai Bloom (Rare):** A miniature bonsai tree in a ceramic pot. When watered with a single drop of the owner's blood, the tree bursts into vibrant, fragrant blossoms for 24 hours. The blossoms have a calming effect on those nearby. Ideal for creating a serene atmosphere. _Price: 300 silver pieces_
    
4. **The Kitsune's Mask (Very Rare):** A beautifully painted mask depicting a cunning fox. While worn, the mask grants the wearer advantage on Deception checks and allows them to speak and understand the Sylvan language. A fascinating piece for those who enjoy a bit of trickery. _Price: 900 silver pieces_
    
5. **The Scroll of Silent Steps (Legendary):** This ancient scroll contains the secrets of moving with supernatural stealth. When read and the proper rituals performed, the scroll grants the reader the ability to move silently for one hour. During this time, they leave no tracks and make no sound, even on difficult terrain. A priceless artifact for the stealthiest of ninjas. _Price: 3,000 silver pieces_
