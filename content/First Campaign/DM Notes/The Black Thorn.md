#### Overview
**A Mercenary Guild:**

- **Ideology:** The Black Thorns are primarily motivated by profit. They are skilled warriors and assassins who sell their services to the highest bidder. They have no real allegiance to any particular cause, except for their own self-interest.
- **Methods:** They take on contracts for assassination, espionage, protection, or any other task that requires their skills. They are ruthless and efficient.
- **Recruitment:** They recruit skilled fighters and those with useful talents. They value competence and discretion.
