World of Thaia: Campaign Setting Overview
Introduction: An Age of Progress and Discontent
Welcome to Thaia, a world teetering on the cusp of revolution, powered by a magical energy source known as Aether. Centuries have passed since the devastating Aetheric War, a conflict that saw the world's magical rulers, the Aetherborn, all but vanish from existence. In their wake, a new civilization of mortals rose, learning to harness the crystallized form of Aether, known as 


Aetherium, to build a new world. 

Today, Thaia is a world of stark contrasts. Grand cities with towering spires and bustling markets are powered by glowing Aetherium stones, and magnificent airships traverse the skies, connecting continents. However, this progress has created a deep class divide.  A powerful, centralized 



World Government presides over the lands from the central continent of Dracordia, while discontent simmers in the shadows. A revolutionary group known as 

The Rising gains followers daily, promising to shatter the old order and create a more equitable world. 

Unbeknownst to the general populace, the ancient powers that once ruled the world are not gone, merely hidden. Descendants of the Aetherborn factions are secretly maneuvering to reclaim their power, manipulating mortal affairs to achieve their ancient goals.  The world is a powder keg, and the players are about to light the fuse.




The Power of Aether

Aether: The fundamental magical energy that flows through the world of Thaia.  It is an almost limitless source of power, the lifeblood of the planet itself. While most mortals cannot interact with raw Aether directly, its presence is felt everywhere.


Aetherium: These are stones of pure, crystallized aetheric power. After the Aetheric War, mortals discovered how to mine and harness Aetherium, using it as a power source for everything from street lamps and industrial machinery to the majestic airships that connect the continents. 



The Aetherborn: In ages past, some individuals were born with the innate ability to command the raw power of Aether, granting them extraordinary abilities. These were the Aetherborn, who were divided into factions based on their philosophies and mastery over different aspects of Aether. After a cataclysmic war nearly drove them to extinction, the Aetherborn faded into myth and legend.  Today, individuals born with these powers are exceedingly rare, often unaware of their own heritage.




Factions and Secret Societies
The World Government: The established ruling body of Thaia, centered in Dracordia. While they have ushered in an age of technological marvels, their rule has fostered a rigid class structure, leading to widespread inequality and resentment. 


The Rising: A growing revolutionary movement dedicated to overthrowing the World Government and dismantling the class system. Led by a mysterious and unidentified figure known only as "The Reaper," their methods are becoming increasingly bold. The Rising is unaware that they are being secretly manipulated by the 


Shadowborn Aetherborn faction to create chaos that will serve as a cover for a much darker agenda. 


The Shadowborn: One of the ancient Aetherborn factions, considered "evil" by their rivals. Operating from the shadows, they seek to reclaim dominion over the world by activating the 

Aetheric Lens, an ancient superweapon used in the original Aetheric War. They are orchestrating the current political instability to achieve their goals. 



The White Lotus: A clandestine organization composed of members from the "good" Aetherborn factions, such as the Sunborn and Whisperwinds. They present themselves as a group fighting against evil, but their primary, secret goal is to stop the Shadowborns and prevent another catastrophic Aetheric war. 


The Lands of Thaia
Dracordia: The heart of civilization and the seat of the World Government's power. This central continent is a hub of commerce, politics, and technology. Its capital, Dragon's Eye, is a marvel of Aetherium-powered engineering, but also a nexus of the world's inequality.

Esari: A historic land in the northwest, known for its ancient institutions and coastal cities like Farshore and Elmsrest. It is a place of learning and old money, where secrets of the Aetherborn might be buried in dusty libraries and forgotten vaults.

Kragheim: A rugged, mountainous land to the west of The Expanse. It is likely the primary source of the world's Aetherium, with deep mines carved into its peaks. The people here are hardy and independent, possibly descendants of the ancient Stonebinders faction.

Jakunta: A vast, dense jungle to the southwest. Considered largely untamed, Jakunta is a land of mystery, dangerous beasts, and rumors of lost cities and potent Aetheric nexuses hidden beneath its thick canopy.

Preya: The fertile breadbasket of the world. This sprawling green land in the south is dotted with farming communities, trade towns, and fortifications to protect its valuable resources.

The Expanse: A wide, open plain that separates the eastern and western landmasses of the main continent. It is a land of nomads, wandering merchants, and the ever-present threat of bandits. Major trade routes cut across its grasslands.

The Bleaks and Skoda: The harsh, frozen north is dominated by the treacherous mountain range known as The Bleaks. Further east lies the desolate, icy peninsula of Skoda, home to only the most resilient and isolated communities, such as Cyperwind Cottage. This region is believed to be the ancestral home of the Whisperwind Aetherborn.

Hammerhead Isles and D'unda: An archipelago off the eastern coast. These islands are havens for sailors, merchants, and pirates alike. Their distance from the central government allows for a greater degree of freedom, and lawlessness.
