### The Aetheric Lens
The overarching idea of this campaign will be to find the build the Aetheric lens.
At the end of the Act, no matter what, the Lens will go off, and destroy the world - creating a waste, ruin like world. 

The group will join the Rising and help with a raid of a government building. Inside the building is a vault, which holds the final piece of the lens. When interacted with, it will set off the lens as a whole, causing the end of the act.


## Act Two
After the Aetheric lens goes off, the world is left in ruins. Smoke and fire fill the majority of the world, and very few survived.
Because of this, many dormant Aetherborns make themselves apparent, and being Aetherborn is a lot more common.
