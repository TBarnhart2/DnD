#### The Cipher: 
The Rising has intercepted encrypted communications between high-ranking government officials. Their codebreakers are slowly deciphering the messages, which seem to refer to something called "Project Aeon." The intercepted snippets hint at the government's knowledge of the Aetherborn and their powers, mentioning terms like "resonance," "confluence," and "legacy." A faction within the Rising, led by a charismatic but ruthless individual, believes Project Aeon is a weapon and wants to seize it for themselves, believing it will guarantee their victory.

#### The Catalyst:
A cell of the Rising, known for its daring raids, targets a government research facility rumored to house ancient artifacts. Their goal is to steal anything that could give them an advantage in their struggle. They succeed in breaching the facility, but they inadvertently trigger a dormant energy field linked to a fragment of the Aetheric Lens. 


#### Project Aeon:
Project Aeon is no longer about weaponizing the Aetherborn power. Instead, it's a containment and research project. The government is desperately trying to understand the Aetheric Lens and find a way to neutralize its power permanently. They know the Lens is incredibly unstable and could reactivate at any time, potentially causing another cataclysm. They are working tirelessly to prevent this, but they are also aware that their efforts might not be enough.

The government fears that the Aetherborn factions are attempting to find pieces of the lens in an attempt to use it to take over the world.
