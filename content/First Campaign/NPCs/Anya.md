Anya is the magic shop owner, who is found at all magic shops around the world. 

- **Appearance:** Anya is a tall, slender figure with long, flowing silver hair that reaches her ankles. It shimmers with an ethereal glow and occasionally seems to drift and swirl even when there's no breeze. Her eyes are large and almond-shaped, constantly shifting through a kaleidoscope of vibrant colors – amethyst, emerald, sapphire, and ruby – like miniature galaxies. Her skin is pale and flawless, with a subtle iridescence that catches the light. She typically wears elegant, high-collared robes of deep indigo silk, embroidered with intricate patterns of constellations and arcane symbols in shimmering silver thread. These patterns seem to subtly rearrange themselves as she moves, as if alive. She often carries a delicate, intricately carved wooden staff topped with a crystal orb that pulses with soft, inner light.
