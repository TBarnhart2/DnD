## Overview
Go to the library to learn more about the lens & what it does
Get attacked by the Aetherborns

#### Before Starting
- You guys can have story between each other, where I basically just mediate (argue!)
- Either roleplay or roll, generally I allow either but bonuses to roleplay
- Lethal vs Non-lethal damage
- Razor

## Library
The library is a university library that only university students and faculty can get in to. You need an ID badge to get in, which you give to a front desk lady to get in. Heimerdinger has an ID to get in, as he is an ex-faculty member

The library is massive, and the book location is unknown (but the name is known)

#### Finding the Book
They can ask the front desk lady for the book location, and she will ask them why (they need a reasonable excuse)
Otherwise other students will know
If they don't ask, it takes 1d20 min to find (if over 10, someone will ask them if they need help)

#### The Book
The book is HUGE, and has info on pretty much anything they wanna know.
"The Luminary Archive: A Historical Analysis of Magical Phenomena"
Written in common, very well taken care of, except for a couple pages with scratched out lines that you might be able to somewhat understand (variable DC wis check).

- Write the info they get from the book
	- Lens works by chaining together Aether crystals that are close by, and multiplies power (DC 20)
	- Pieces will magically connect together when in close proximity, and touching small stone activates it (DC 15)
	- Super effective damage against people w/o Aetherborn abilities (DC 20)
	- Aether war saw 95% of the world wiped out, likely similar here (DC 5)
	- Aether is generally passed through genetics (DC 10), but rarely can be activated (DC 15)
	- There are more than 4 Aetherborn groups (DC 25)

###### Encounter 1: Heimerdinger
While looking at/for the book, Heimerdinger will spot them and approach, asking what they're doing
- Depending on how they act, he might help them or get them kicked out
- Heimerdinger is neutral to the Rising, but he needs to be sure that they aren't working for the war aetherborns (not favorable to the Rising, personally thinks it's small potatoes to the Aetherborn war)

###### Encounter 2: Flirty
A girl/guy will approach them and flirt with them, asking what they're up to and where they're from (this includes which dorm building they're in)


###### Encounter 3: Razor (Elon Musk)
Razor will show up and try to take the book (he is allowed in, actually welcomed)
- He is a huge donor to the university

The librarian will go look for the book, and if not found on the shelf, she will make an intercom announcement asking whoever has it to bring it up to the counter.

#### Leaving the Library
The book (and pages) will shriek extremely loudly if taken outside of the library

Destroying it requires magical fire

Hoping walls is best call if they can, otherwise run!
